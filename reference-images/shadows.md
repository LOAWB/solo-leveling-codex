## Notes for shadows

- **Universal shadow visual language:**
  - Light purple translucent body or aura
  - Glowing eyes (color varies by shadow)
  - Smoky-edge details where solid matter meets shadow
  - Each retains pre-shadow distinctive visual but tinted purple

- **Shadow tier visual signaling:**
  - Soldiers: small, dark, swarm-tier silhouettes
  - Knights: full-armored, faceplate-helmet, mid-tier presence
  - Generals (Igris, Beru, Tusk, Iron): named units with distinctive personal identifying features
  - Marshals (Bellion, post-evolution Beru, post-evolution Igris): cosmic-tier presence, deeper aura, ornate weaponry

- **Avoid:** making them look like generic shadow ghosts. Each retains personality and distinctive trait from pre-shadow form
- **Loyalty signaling:** shadows often shown in formal/respectful poses around Jinwoo, never casual
