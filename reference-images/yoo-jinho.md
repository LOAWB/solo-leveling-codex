## Notes for Pory

- Yoo Jinho's defining visuals: glasses, dark messy-then-styled hair, transitions from nervous A-rank to confident Guild Master across the arc
- Color palette: blues, grays (armor), brown hair, gentle warm tones for civilian
- Personality cues: respectful, loyal, brick-by-brick discipline, second-son-of-conglomerate underestimated, audience surrogate for ordinary humans in the hunter world
- Combat role: tank-class with shield, defensive specialist, not a striker
- Avoid: making him too generic-anime-side-character. He has specific "earned-his-place" arc weight
- Glasses are a defining detail; almost always present
- Posture evolution: hunched-nervous in early canon, confident-administrative in late canon
