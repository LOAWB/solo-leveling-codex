## Notes for Pory

- Park Kyung-Hye's defining visuals: middle-aged Korean woman, dark hair (slight gray), kind motherly face, warm presence
- Color palette: soft warm browns, pastel domestic colors, white (hospital state)
- Personality cues: kind protective mother, was-sick-now-healed, central anchor of family safety, civilian
- Two major states: comatose Eternal-Slumber state vs healthy post-cure state
- Avoid: making her look generic-anime-mom. She's specifically Korean middle-aged with weight of her illness
- Almost always pair her with family context, never alone in dramatic combat scenes
