## Notes for Pory

- Cha Hae-In's defining visual: short bright blonde hair, golden eyes, white-and-silver sword-class uniform with gold accents
- Always pair with elegance: she's a sword-class S-rank with poise, not a brawler
- Mana Sense moments: soft gold-tinted aura, eyes nearly closed
- Color palette: white, silver, gold, soft pastels
- Avoid: hyper-sexualized outfits or poses, generic anime-girl stylization. She's a top-tier hunter with dignity
- Bond moments with Jinwoo: subtle blush, soft warm lighting, understated emotion
- Combat moments: precise, controlled, not chaotic
