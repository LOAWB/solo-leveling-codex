## Notes for Pory

- Sung Jinwoo's defining visual: sharp facial features, glowing purple eyes when in System or combat states, dark coats with hood, twin daggers as primary weapon
- Color palette: black, deep purple, gold accent (System), pale skin tone
- Avoid: anime-tropey big colorful eyes, generic shounen hero look. He should feel grounded-cool, not flashy
- Compare to: Sasuke Uchiha brooding tone but with more compact dagger-class kit, less flowing
- Bond Forge mode (with allies present) softens his expression slightly
- Shadow Monarch peak should feel cosmic, not just dark
