## Notes for Pory

- Beru's defining visuals: humanoid ant warrior, neon yellow eyes (CRITICAL detail), light purple translucent body, smoky purple wings, sharp curved claws
- Color palette: purple, dark grays, neon yellow accent (eyes), pink-purple shadow tints
- Personality cues: cult-like devotion to Jinwoo, hypersonic execution speed, broken-English speech ("My King"), surprisingly gentle around protected family members
- Power tiers: pre-shadow Ant King is brown-armored hostile; post-shadow Beru is purple-translucent reverent
- Avoid: making him look like a generic monster. He has dignified imperial bearing despite being insectoid
- Eye color must always be neon yellow, never red (red is the pre-shadow Ant King)
