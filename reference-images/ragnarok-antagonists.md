## Notes for Ragnarok antagonists

- **Tiel:** Dual-form character (Apostle true / Park Dojin disguise). Cosmic-alien aesthetic vs cool-corporate-hunter.
- **Brocky:** Three-state arc (Rakan-loyal / brainwashed / shadow-revived). Beast-warrior across all states with eye-color shifts.
- **Lee Minsung:** Two-face character (public actor / private villain). Charm and cruelty layered.
- **Arsha:** Dual-form (human nightclub-owner / Queen of Bees). Modern-Korean-elegance vs alien-insect-royalty.
- **Esil Radiru:** Cross-canon support. High Elf healer aesthetic.

- **Color palettes:**
  - Tiel: dark-and-gold (Apostle), professional black (Park Dojin)
  - Brocky: brown-and-black (beast), purple aura when brainwashed, restored after revival
  - Lee Minsung: charcoal-and-red (charming actor), dark-purple corrupt (post-Stardust)
  - Arsha: black-and-gold (cocktail bar), yellow-and-black (Queen of Bees)
  - Esil Radiru: white + gold + dark blue (High Elf)

- **Avoid:** making them look like generic anime villains. Each has specific Ragnarok-arc weight and visual identity beyond their name
