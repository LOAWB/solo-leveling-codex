## Notes for Pory

- Sung Suho's defining visuals: combination of father (dark hair, sharp features) and mother (GOLDEN eyes - critical detail), modern Korean teen
- Color palette: dark hair, golden eyes (mother's), red-gold for inherited Destruction power, modern teen fashion
- Personality cues: starts strong by genealogy, sealed-then-awakened, normal school life context, becomes Heir of Destruction
- Power state: gold eyes always (mother's mana sense passive); red-gold cosmic aura when channeling Antares fragment
- Avoid: making him look like a clone of Jinwoo. He has his mother's eyes, more modern teen vibe, less brooding
- Age range: child (6-8) for sealed flashbacks, teenager (15-17) for main Ragnarok narrative
