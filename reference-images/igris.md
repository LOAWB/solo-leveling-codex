## Notes for Pory

- Igris's defining visuals: tall imposing knight in RED-AND-GOLD plate armor, helmet always closed in canon (helmet-off is rare/fan reveal), longsword as primary weapon, dark crimson cape
- Color palette: deep red, gold, dark purple (post-shadow), pale skin tone (for rare helmet-off)
- Personality cues: stoic loyal warrior, never disobeys Jinwoo, knight's-honor archetype, refuses dishonorable plays
- Power tiers: pre-shadow Knight Commander has red eyes; shadow form has purple eyes
- Knight Killer evolution: more refined armor, double cape, ornate sword
- Avoid: making him look like a generic fantasy knight. He has aristocratic regal bearing
- Pose typically formal or in motion: never casual or relaxed
