## Notes for all Monarchs

- **Faction-coded color palettes:**
  - Antares: black + crimson
  - Ashborn: black + purple (or white + silver in Ruler form)
  - Baruka (Frost): pale blue + white + ice crystal
  - Rakan (Beasts): brown + black + animal-skull
  - Tarnak (Iron Body): grey + iron-textured + yellow accents
  - Yogumunt (Transfiguration): smoky black + ghostly white + transparent
  - Querehsha (Plagues): green + dark gold + insect-segment
  - Legia (Beginning, Giants): deep blue + white-edge robes (martial-arts theme)
  - Baran (Demons): red skin + dark armor + lightning crackle

- **Universal Monarch traits:**
  - Cosmic-tier presence (always feel larger than human Hunters)
  - Distinctive horn shapes per Monarch
  - Glowing eye color matched to faction
  - Ornate armor or robes specific to their domain
  - Royal/imperial bearing (they are kings of their respective monster armies)

- **Avoid:** generic-fantasy-villain look. Each Monarch has a specific theme that distinguishes them. Lean into the distinctive faction iconography.
