## Notes for Pory

- Ashborn's defining visuals: TWO FORMS - (1) Monarch of Shadows: PURPLE FLAME HAIR, two black horns, jet-black armor, smoky cloak, black sword. (2) Original Ruler: white feathered wings, silver hair, white robes, white sword.
- Color palette: Monarch form = BLACK + DEEP PURPLE + flame highlights. Ruler form = WHITE + SILVER + GOLD with light blue cloak.
- Personality cues: ancient cosmic being, mentor to Jinwoo, tragically betrayed, last Ruler-loyal-to-Absolute-Being, willingly merges with Jinwoo at the end
- Critical: he is NOT a villain. He's an antagonist-tier being who allies with Jinwoo and sacrifices himself
- Two forms must be visually distinguished. Don't blend them
- Scale: cosmic-tier presence matching Antares
- Avoid: making him look like generic dark lord. He has specific Solo-Leveling-cosmic-grandfather feel
