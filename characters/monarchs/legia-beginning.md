# Legia

**Wiki:** https://solo-leveling.fandom.com/wiki/Legia
**Title:** Monarch of the Beginning, King of Giants
**Tier:** S+
**Army:** Giants

## Bio

The Monarch of the Beginning, King of the Giants. Spatial dimension distortion specialist; can travel between dimensional rifts and has extraordinary martial arts skill. Like other Monarchs he holds humanity in contempt and seeks their extinction.

## Abilities

- **Spatial dimension distortion**: can travel between rifts at will.
- **Martial arts mastery**: physical combat skill peerless among Monarchs.
- **Giants army command**.

## Strength notes

- Mid-tier Monarch.
- Mobility advantage: hardest Monarch to pin down.

## Development arc

- **Monarchs War Arc**: deploys Giants. Uses dimensional travel to stay elusive.
- **Final Battle**: caught and killed during the war.

## Game-design hooks

- Mobility-tier boss: hard to engage, has to be cornered. Tracking-mechanic design rather than damage-race.
- Martial arts class on cosmic-tier enemy: melee precision at SS scale.
