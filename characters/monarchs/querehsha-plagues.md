# Querehsha

**Wiki:** https://solo-leveling.fandom.com/wiki/Querehsha
**Title:** Monarch of Plagues, Queen of Insects
**Tier:** S+
**Army:** Insects

## Bio

The Insect Queen. The only female Monarch. Bold appearance and a problematic nature. She survives the longest in early Monarchs War engagements because of her plague mechanics: stalling, attrition, denial. Unlike Antares (burst) or Rakan (melee dominance), she wins by wearing the field down over time.

## Abilities

- **Plague Mist**: corrosive field that wears down everything it touches.
- **Insect army command**: swarm tactics.
- **Disease propagation**: lingering effects.

## Strength notes

- Mid-tier Monarch.
- Strongest in extended engagements; weak in burst trades.
- Survives early skirmishes longer than other Monarchs.

## Development arc

- **Monarchs War Arc**: deploys Plague Mist on multiple fronts.
- **Final Battle**: killed during the war.

## Game-design hooks

- Attrition boss design: damage-over-time threat where the fight is about endurance, not burst response.
- Female Monarch as the sole rep: gender variance at the apex tier without making it a feature beyond appearance.
