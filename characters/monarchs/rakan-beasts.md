# Rakan

**Wiki:** https://solo-leveling.fandom.com/wiki/Rakan
**Title:** Monarch of Beastly Fangs, King of Beasts, Beast Monarch
**Tier:** SS
**Army:** Beasts

## Bio

The Beast Monarch. Tall, dark-skinned, muscular humanoid form with sharp teeth. Pure-physical apex among Monarchs. He is the closest Monarch to Antares in raw power among the non-cosmic-class Monarchs (Antares and Ashborn-prime sit alone above).

Defining trait: bloodthirsty. He butchers enemies without hesitation. Tactical depth low; ferocity high.

## Abilities

- **Beast Authority**: command of the King of Beasts faction (giant feral creatures).
- **Hypersonic combat speed**.
- **Regeneration**.
- **Threat level: Dragon** (canonical second-highest classification, just under Antares' Demon God).

## Strength notes

- Co-strongest non-Antares Monarch with Yogumunt.
- Pure-physical fighter; outclassed only by raw cosmic-tier power.
- Loses to fully-ascended Jinwoo.

## Development arc

- **Monarchs War Arc**: leads attacks on multiple international National Levels.
- **Final Battle**: dies during the Monarchs' culling phase. Antares may absorb his power.

## Game-design hooks

- Pure-melee apex: contrast to Antares' all-tools kit. Same tier, different role.
- Bloodthirsty AI archetype: aggressive auto-attack pattern with low pattern-readability.
