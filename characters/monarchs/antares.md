# Antares

**Wiki:** https://solo-leveling.fandom.com/wiki/Antares
**Title:** Monarch of Destruction, King of Berserk Dragons, Dragon Emperor
**Tier:** SS / Final Boss
**Army:** Dragons

## Bio

The strongest Monarch and the final boss of Solo Leveling. King of the Berserk Dragons. Wields a dragon-scale longsword. Among the nine Monarchs he is the apex; every other Monarch fears him. Pre-Jinwoo's full awakening, Antares is the strongest entity in canon.

His final form fights Jinwoo (post-Ashborn merge) outside Korea in the Final Battle Arc. Despite absorbing the residual power of every other Monarch as fuel, he loses. Jinwoo defeats him twice (once in the original timeline's final confrontation, once again after Antares attempts to return).

## Abilities

- **Dragon Emperor's Roar**: freezes anything in an instant; even S-ranks have convulsions.
- **Hypersonic flight and combat speed**.
- **Indelible body**: extreme durability.
- **Spatial manipulation**: highest among Monarchs.
- **Regeneration negation**: can deny opponents' healing.
- **Threat level: Demon God** (canonical highest classification).
- **Dragon-scale longsword**.
- **Dragon army command**.

## Strength notes

- Strongest Monarch by raw output.
- Equal-or-above Ashborn at prime.
- Loses to Jinwoo at full Shadow Monarch ascension.
- Without Antares, the Monarchs War would already be lost for the Monarchs side; he is their decisive piece.

## Development arc

- **Pre-canon**: established as oldest and strongest Monarch.
- **Monarchs War**: orchestrates the final assault.
- **Final Battle**: solo confrontation with Jinwoo. Burns Jinwoo's left hand. Loses.
- **Return attempt**: tries to manifest again. Killed permanently.

## Game-design hooks

- Final-boss kit composition: every Monarch ability he has is a dialed-up version of another Monarch's. Antares is the cumulative-buff boss design.
- Two-phase final boss: defeat once, recurrence as a second phase. Game-design template for "did you actually finish him?" tension.
- Authority that breaks game rules: Roar that freezes enemies, regeneration negation. Final boss has access to mechanics no other entity does.
