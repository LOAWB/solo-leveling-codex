# Tarnak

**Wiki:** https://solo-leveling.fandom.com/wiki/Tarnak
**Title:** Monarch of the Iron Body, King of Monstrous Humanoids
**Tier:** S+
**Army:** Iron Golems / Monstrous Humanoids

## Bio

The Iron Body Monarch. King of nearly indestructible Golems. Energy manipulation specialist with stat-enhancement buffs that he can stack on his army. Mighty and arrogant, he holds humanity in particular contempt because he sees them as soft creatures unworthy of his attention.

## Abilities

- **Iron Body**: extreme physical durability personally.
- **Stat enhancement**: buffs his army's stats.
- **Energy manipulation**.
- **Golem army command**.

## Strength notes

- Mid-tier Monarch.
- Below Antares, Ashborn-prime, Rakan, Yogumunt.
- Above Baruka, Querehsha, Legia.

## Development arc

- **Monarchs War Arc**: deploys Golem army.
- **Final Battle context**: dies during the war.

## Game-design hooks

- Buff-aura boss: not a striker, a force-multiplier. Killing him weakens his army significantly.
- Tank-tier Monarch: durable solo fighter; war-of-attrition design.
