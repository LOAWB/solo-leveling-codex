# Baran

**Wiki:** https://solo-leveling.fandom.com/wiki/Baran
**Title:** Monarch of White Flames, King of Demons
**Tier:** S+ (sometimes listed at SS depending on source)
**Army:** Demons

## Bio

The Demon Monarch. King of Demons. Lightning manipulation specialist with dimensional storage that lets him summon nasty demons on command. Among Monarchs he has one of the most varied combat kits because his army is itself diverse.

He notably appeared early in canon during the Demonic Castle Dungeon (Jinwoo's mid-progression dungeon) as a higher-power threat that influenced the trial. The dungeon was a Monarch-tier-leveled environment.

## Abilities

- **Lightning manipulation**: high-burst attack.
- **Dimensional storage**: summons demons mid-fight.
- **Demon army command**.
- **High mana reserves**.

## Strength notes

- Mid-tier Monarch.
- Versatile kit through summoning options.
- Killed during the Monarchs War.

## Development arc

- **Demonic Castle Dungeon (early canon)**: thematic presence; Jinwoo encounters a Demonic Castle that derived from his domain.
- **Monarchs War**: deploys Demon army. Killed during the war.

## Game-design hooks

- Summoner-class Monarch: combat kit by army composition rather than personal moveset. Adaptive boss fights based on what he summons.
- Lightning-element apex: status-effect-heavy versus physical-damage-heavy contrast.
