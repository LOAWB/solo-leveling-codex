# Yogumunt

**Wiki:** https://solo-leveling.fandom.com/wiki/Yogumunt
**Title:** Monarch of Transfiguration, King of Demonic Spectres
**Tier:** SS (tied with Rakan as second-strongest non-Antares Monarch)
**Army:** Demonic Spectres / Shapeshifters

## Bio

The Monarch of Transfiguration. King of Demonic Spectres. Tied with Rakan as the second-strongest Monarch behind Antares. Hypersonic speed, regeneration, threat level Dragon. Specializes in shapeshifting and battlefield deception.

He coordinates the targeted attacks on National Level Hunters during the Monarchs War, including the assault on Christopher Reed.

## Abilities

- **Transfiguration**: shapeshifting at extreme scale; can assume any form including faking allies.
- **Hypersonic speed**.
- **Regeneration**.
- **Spectres army command**.
- **Threat level: Dragon**.

## Strength notes

- Co-second-strongest Monarch with Rakan.
- Most strategically valuable Monarch: deception lets him flank and ambush.
- Targets the strongest humans first.

## Development arc

- **Monarchs War Arc**: leads coordinated National-Level assault.
- **Final Battle**: dies during the war.

## Game-design hooks

- Shapeshift boss: can disguise as friendly NPCs. Trust mechanic threat.
- Hunter-of-bosses: targets the strongest first. Reverses normal aggression patterns.
