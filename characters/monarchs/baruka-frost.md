# Baruka

**Wiki:** https://solo-leveling.fandom.com/wiki/Baruka
**Title:** Monarch of Frost, King of Snow Folk
**Also known as:** Arte (some translations)
**Tier:** S+
**Army:** Snow Folk (frost-giants)

## Bio

The Frost Monarch. King of the Snow Folk. Known for tactical precision: every move he makes is pre-calculated. He plays the long game, picking targets with surgical intent rather than bursting into chaos like Antares or Rakan.

He is the Monarch who personally kills Korean Hunter Association Chairman Go Gunhee in the Monarchs War, marking the formal start of the global Monarchs vs Hunters war.

## Abilities

- **Frost authority**: ice generation, freezing field control.
- **Snow Folk command**: army of frost-giants.
- **Tactical foresight**: pre-calculates every move.

## Strength notes

- Mid-tier Monarch.
- Solo kills Go Gunhee but loses to coalition response.
- Below Antares, Yogumunt, Rakan in raw power.

## Development arc

- **Monarchs War Arc**: kills Go Gunhee in Seoul.
- **Final Battle context**: dies during the Monarchs' final assault, either at Jinwoo's hands or absorbed by Antares for power.

## Game-design hooks

- Calculator boss archetype: chess-master rather than berserker. Pattern-readable but precise.
- AOE-control authority: frost is battlefield denial more than DPS.
