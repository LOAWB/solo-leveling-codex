# Beru

**Wiki:** https://solo-leveling.fandom.com/wiki/Beru
**Title:** Ant King → Marshal of Shadows
**Tier:** SS (post-evolution)
**Origin:** Jeju Island Ant King

## Bio

Originally the Ant King of Jeju Island, the boss who killed Goto Ryuji and nearly killed Cha Hae-In. Defeated by Jinwoo at the climax of Jeju Island Arc and extracted as a shadow. Beru evolves rapidly under Jinwoo's command, becoming the most overpowered individual unit in his army.

Beru is the chaos-aligned counterpart to Igris's lawful-aligned discipline. He worships Jinwoo with cult-like devotion, executes enemies with grotesque precision, and addresses Jinwoo as "my king" in awkward broken English (a fan-favorite trait).

In post-Cup-of-Reincarnation timeline, Jinwoo gives a weakened Beru to his son Sung Suho as the boy's partner shadow.

## Appearance

Huge humanoid shadow ant with light purple glow, neon eyes, sharp claws, smoky wings.

## Abilities

- **Hypersonic speed**: fastest unit in Jinwoo's army.
- **Execution finishing moves**: dismembers high-tier enemies.
- **King's Authority** (over lesser ants): can command Magic Beast ants.
- **Healing acid**: contradictory mix of toxin and restoration.
- **Wings**: aerial mobility.

## Strength notes

- Solos S-ranks.
- Comparable to Igris-Knight-Killer in raw output, surpasses him in speed and execution.
- Force-multiplier in coalition fights against Monarchs.

## Development arc

- **Pre-shadow**: Ant King of Jeju Island.
- **Defeat**: killed by Jinwoo at Jeju.
- **As shadow**: rapid evolution, becomes top-3 shadow.
- **Post-canon**: weakened form given to Suho as partner shadow.

## Game-design hooks

- Boss-converted-to-companion: this is the keystone Arise mechanic moment in canon. Game design: every memorable boss should be considered for a "becomes ally" path.
- Personality flip: monstrous before, devoted after. Same model, different alignment, dramatically different character voice.
- Inheritable companion: parent-protagonist's companion passes diluted to child. Generational gear pattern.
