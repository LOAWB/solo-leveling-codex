# Greed

**Wiki:** https://solo-leveling.fandom.com/wiki/Greed
**Title:** Former Knight Commander Shadow
**Tier:** S
**Origin:** Inherited from Ashborn

## Bio

Another high-rank shadow inherited from Ashborn's original army. Greed was a former Knight Commander whose archetype overlaps with Igris's. He is sometimes listed as a parallel general to Igris in Jinwoo's command structure post-merge.

## Abilities

- **Heavy weapon mastery**.
- **Knight-tier combat technique**.

## Strength notes

- Parallel to Igris pre-Knight-Killer evolution.
- Inherited unit, not earned via boss kill.

## Development arc

- **Post-Ashborn merge**: awakens.
- **Final Battle**: deployed alongside other inherited shadows.

## Game-design hooks

- Roster redundancy at the same role: two Knight-class generals in the same army. Validates depth-by-redundancy in roster design rather than forced uniqueness.
