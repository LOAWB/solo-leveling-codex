# Tank

**Wiki:** https://solo-leveling.fandom.com/wiki/Tank
**Title:** Wolf Shadow / Hellhound
**Tier:** A
**Origin:** Hellhound / Cerberus boss

## Bio

A wolf-class shadow with hellhound traits. Tank fills scout, light cavalry, and beast-guard roles. Smaller in scale than Iron/Igris/Beru but has utility as a fast-moving beast unit.

Note: confusingly named "Tank" despite functioning as a fast scout, not a damage absorber. The actual tank-role shadow in Jinwoo's army is Iron.

## Abilities

- **Speed**: fast ground movement.
- **Beast-form combat**.
- **Scout instincts**.

## Strength notes

- Lower combat tier.
- High mobility.

## Development arc

- **Mid-canon**: extracted.
- **Standard background deployment**.

## Game-design hooks

- Naming-convention reminder: don't let unit names dictate role. "Tank" the wolf is a scout, "Iron" is the actual tank.
