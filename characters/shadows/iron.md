# Iron

**Wiki:** https://solo-leveling.fandom.com/wiki/Iron
**Title:** Gate Paladin Shadow
**Tier:** A+ → S
**Origin:** Steel Gate boss

## Bio

A heavy-armor tank shadow extracted from a powerful gate-guardian. Iron specializes in tanking and peeling: he holds the line and pulls aggro off Jinwoo's allies. His shield-and-sword kit makes him the formal tank line of Jinwoo's army.

## Abilities

- **Heavy shield defense**.
- **Aggro pull / threat generation**.
- **Sword-and-shield combat**.

## Strength notes

- Mid-shadow tier.
- Defensive role rather than offensive striker.
- Force-multiplier in extended raids by absorbing damage.

## Development arc

- **Mid-canon**: extracted as shadow.
- **Standard deployment** through to Final Battle.

## Game-design hooks

- Tank-line shadow: roster role-coverage with explicit tank/healer/DPS/CC slots, even in a "summoned unit" army.
