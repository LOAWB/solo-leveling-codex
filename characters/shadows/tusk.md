# Tusk

**Wiki:** https://solo-leveling.fandom.com/wiki/Tusk
**Title:** High Ogre Shadow / Mage-class
**Tier:** S
**Origin:** High Orc Mage / Ogre Shaman boss

## Bio

A mage-class shadow extracted from a powerful magic beast. Tusk fills the AOE crowd-control role in Jinwoo's army. Where Igris is melee precision and Beru is single-target execution, Tusk is wide-area damage and elemental burst.

## Abilities

- **Magic blasts**: AOE damage.
- **Elemental authority**: lightning, ice, fire.
- **Crowd control**: stuns and area denial.

## Strength notes

- S-tier shadow.
- Critical for raid scenarios with multiple weaker enemies.
- Force multiplier against monster armies, less impactful against single-S-rank threats.

## Development arc

- **Mid-canon**: extracted as shadow.
- **Ahjin Guild Arc onward**: standard deployment.
- **Final Battle**: full deployment.

## Game-design hooks

- Caster-class shadow: fills the AOE niche that melee shadows can't. Roster role-coverage design.
- Elemental versatility on a shadow rather than the protagonist: kit diversity through summoned units.
