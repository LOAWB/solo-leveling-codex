# Kaisel

**Wiki:** https://solo-leveling.fandom.com/wiki/Kaisel
**Title:** Wyvern Shadow
**Tier:** A+
**Origin:** Wyvern boss

## Bio

A wyvern-class shadow. Kaisel provides aerial mobility for Jinwoo and aerial striking. He is more often used as Jinwoo's personal mount than as a striker, though he can deal damage in flight.

## Abilities

- **Flight**: high-altitude mobility.
- **Aerial strikes**: dive attacks.
- **Mount-class**: carries Jinwoo or other allies.

## Strength notes

- Lower combat tier than Igris/Beru/Tusk.
- High utility tier for mobility.

## Development arc

- **Mid-canon**: extracted.
- **Standard deployment**: typically as transport/recon.

## Game-design hooks

- Mount class: not a striker. Utility-driven shadow design.
- Distinct from striker shadows: roster diversity by role rather than power.
