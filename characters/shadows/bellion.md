# Bellion

**Wiki:** https://solo-leveling.fandom.com/wiki/Bellion
**Title:** Marshal of the Shadows
**Tier:** S+ → SS
**Origin:** Ashborn's army (inherited)

## Bio

Bellion was the original Marshal of Ashborn's shadow army. When Jinwoo merges with Ashborn and inherits the Shadow Monarch position, he also inherits Bellion. Pre-merge, Bellion is in stasis. Post-merge, he is awakened as Jinwoo's strongest shadow general before Beru's full evolution.

Bellion's strength is so significant that pre-merge Jinwoo would lose to him. He represents the cosmic-tier scale Ashborn operated on.

## Abilities

- **Extreme reach**: scythe / massive weapon.
- **Leadership of the original shadow legion**.
- **Authority-tier shadow combat**.

## Strength notes

- Strongest individual shadow inherited from Ashborn.
- Comparable to a National Level Hunter pre-Beru-evolution; surpassed by Beru post-evolution.

## Development arc

- **Pre-merge**: in stasis.
- **Post-Ashborn merge**: awakens. Joins Jinwoo's command structure.
- **Final Battle**: deployed against Antares' army.

## Game-design hooks

- Inherited-on-evolution roster: when the protagonist class evolves, new units unlock not from quest but from class-power pool.
- Sleeping general archetype: introduced post-class-up rather than via boss kill.
