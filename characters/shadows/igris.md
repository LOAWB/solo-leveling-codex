# Igris

**Wiki:** https://solo-leveling.fandom.com/wiki/Igris
**Title:** Blood-Red Knight Commander → Knight Killer
**Korean:** 이그리트
**Tier:** S → S+ (post-evolution)
**Origin:** Cartenon Temple boss

## Bio

The first named Shadow extracted by Jinwoo. Igris was originally the Blood-Red Knight Commander of an ancient kingdom, defeated by Jinwoo at the end of the job-change quest in the Cartenon Temple. He becomes the first general of Jinwoo's shadow army and serves as Jinwoo's right-hand man.

Igris is the most disciplined of the shadows: knight's-honor archetype, refuses tactically dishonorable plays, fights with peerless swordplay. He evolves into the Knight Killer form during the Red Gate Arc, scaling further as Jinwoo levels.

## Abilities

- **Master swordsmanship**: peerless among Jinwoo's shadows.
- **Knight Killer evolution**: post-Red Gate, gains anti-knight specialization and significant stat increase.
- **Loyalty-locked**: never disobeys Jinwoo.

## Strength notes

- Top-tier shadow general before Beru's evolution.
- Solos S-rank threats.
- Continues to scale with Jinwoo's progression.

## Development arc

- **Cartenon Temple**: defeated by Jinwoo. Becomes first major shadow.
- **Red Gate**: evolves into Knight Killer.
- **Jeju onward**: anchor shadow of Jinwoo's vanguard.
- **Final Battle**: full-strength deployment.

## Game-design hooks

- First-companion archetype: introduced early, scales with the protagonist permanently. Cannot be replaced without narrative cost.
- Evolution-on-trigger: Igris's transformation is event-driven (Red Gate completion), not level-driven. Quest-gated evolution mechanic.
