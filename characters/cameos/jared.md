# Jared

**Status:** Cameo / side character (non-canon Solo Leveling, original to this codex)
**Rank:** A-Rank rising to S-Rank
**Class:** Bond Hunter (rare perception-class)
**Nation:** USA (West Coast, California)
**Affiliation:** Independent operator, founder of a small guild "by the brick"

## Bio

Jared awakened late, several years into the Gate era, while working a full-time job he refused to quit until his hunter income could replace it. Most awakeners go straight into a major guild on day one; Jared spent a year working day shifts and raiding by night, learning the ground floor of the hunter economy from the inside before letting anyone recruit him.

He doesn't have the raw mana ceiling of a National Level, but he reads people the way Cha Hae-In reads mana. His passive, **Resonance Sense**, lets him feel the intent of any sentient being within range. He can tell when an ally is hiding fatigue, when an enemy is bluffing strength, when a guild master is about to betray a contract. The ability was originally classified as social, but in combat it has the side effect of letting him pre-empt attacks half a beat before they land.

He built his guild brick by brick. No flagship raid. No National Level cosign. He picked one steady B-rank carwash-territory clearance contract, ran it cleanly for sixty days, and let the work speak. By the time other guilds noticed, he had a roster of mid-rank hunters who chose him because he didn't ask them to die for ambition.

## Abilities

- **Resonance Sense (passive)**: feels the emotional/intent state of any sentient within ~50 meters. Pre-empts attacks. Detects deception.
- **Bond Forge** (active): when fighting alongside a hunter he has bonded with through repeated combat, his stats and theirs gain a shared multiplier. Solo, his stats are A-rank. With two trusted bond-partners present, S-rank.
- **Brick Discipline**: extreme stamina under repeated mid-tier engagement. Excels in marathon raids where fresh-S-ranks burn out.
- **Sword and shield**: standard fighter loadout. Unflashy, well-maintained.

## Strength notes

- Solo: high A-rank.
- With two bond-partners: mid-S-rank.
- His ceiling is collaborative, not individual.
- Loses to single-S-rank-and-above without his crew.

## Development arc (in-universe placement)

- **Pre-canon (Solo Leveling timeline)**: building his guild. Background presence in the West Coast hunter scene.
- **During Monarchs War**: deploys his bonded crew to defend civilian zones. Holds a coastal city block.
- **Post-Cup-of-Reincarnation**: in the new timeline, Jared never awakens (no Gates). He runs a small business successfully, applying the same brick-by-brick discipline to civilian industry.

## Game-design hooks

- **Bond-amplifier class**: stats scale with relationship state, not raw level. Useful design template for relationship-driven RPG mechanics.
- **Late-awakener archetype**: most stories awaken protagonists at the start. Jared awakens with adult life context already in place. Models a more grounded protagonist arc.
- **Guild-as-character-development**: his progression isn't his stats, it's his roster. Game-design: levels you up by who chooses to fight beside you.
