# Baek Yoonho

**Wiki:** https://solo-leveling.fandom.com/wiki/Baek_Yoonho
**Rank:** S
**Class:** Beast-type / Transformation S-Rank
**Korean:** 백윤호
**Epithet:** White Tiger
**Guild:** White Tiger Guild Master

## Bio

One of Korea's senior S-rank hunters and the Master of the White Tiger Guild. Beast-type awakener with full-body transformation into a giant white tiger form. Pragmatic, loyal, sees Jinwoo's potential earlier than most other S-ranks. He's one of the first to accept Jinwoo as a peer rather than treat him with suspicion.

## Abilities

- **White Tiger Transformation**: full-body shapeshift into apex-predator form. Strength + speed + durability all multiplied.
- **Hybrid form**: partial transformation (claws, increased musculature) for finesse combat.
- **Veteran combat sense**: decades of raiding experience.

## Strength notes

- Top-tier Korean S-rank, second-highest by combat output behind Cha Hae-In.
- Cannot solo a Monarch but contributes meaningfully to multi-S-rank engagements.
- Held the line during Seoul incursion in Monarchs War.

## Development arc

- **Pre-canon**: established Master of White Tiger Guild.
- **Mid-canon**: meets Jinwoo, recognizes his strength early.
- **Monarchs War**: deployed in Seoul defense alongside other S-ranks.
- **Final Battle**: leads Korean contingent globally.
- **Epilogue**: post-reset, would not awaken without Gates.

## Game-design hooks

- Transformation class as cooldown-economy: full-form is high-power, time-limited. Mid-form is sustained but weaker. Two-mode resource design.
- Senior NPC archetype who validates protagonist before peers do.
