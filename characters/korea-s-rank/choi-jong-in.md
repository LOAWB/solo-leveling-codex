# Choi Jong-In

**Wiki:** https://solo-leveling.fandom.com/wiki/Choi_Jong-In
**Rank:** S
**Class:** Mage / Fire-type S-Rank
**Korean:** 최종인
**Guild:** Hunters Guild Master

## Bio

Master of the Hunters Guild, one of Korea's largest. Korea's top mage-class S-rank specializing in fire magic. Known for being intelligent, cautious, and politically savvy; he runs his guild with corporate discipline. Cha Hae-In was his Vice-Master before joining Ahjin.

## Abilities

- **Fire authority**: high-volume flame attacks with precision targeting.
- **Mana reserve**: among the highest sustained-cast S-ranks in Korea.
- **Strategic command**: leads multi-team raids effectively.

## Strength notes

- Korea's strongest mage-class.
- Acknowledged as weaker than his own Vice-Master Cha Hae-In in raw combat.
- A-tier in the global S-rank conversation.

## Development arc

- **Pre-canon**: founded and led Hunters Guild.
- **Hunters Guild Gate Arc**: leads the rescue raid where Jinwoo is encountered.
- **Recruitment Arc**: tries to retain Cha Hae-In; loses her to Ahjin.
- **Monarchs War**: leads Hunters Guild contingent in Seoul.
- **Final Battle**: deployed internationally.
- **Epilogue**: in new timeline never awakens.

## Game-design hooks

- Mage-class specialist: pure caster archetype with no melee tools.
- Guild Master administrator + active raider: dual-role NPC pattern.
