# Min Byung-Gyu

**Wiki:** https://solo-leveling.fandom.com/wiki/Min_Byung-Gyu
**Rank:** S
**Class:** Healer-type S-Rank

## Bio

Korea's only S-rank healer. Disproportionately valuable: an S-rank healer is rarer than an S-rank striker because healing-class awakeners almost never reach S. He keeps multi-S-rank raids alive in extended engagements.

## Abilities

- **Group heal**: full-team healing under combat pressure.
- **High-grade revives**: not full-resurrection but can stabilize fatal wounds quickly.

## Strength notes

- Combat-weak as a striker.
- Force-multiplier for any raid he supports.
- Critical for the Korean coalition's survival rate during Monarchs War.

## Development arc

- Background but consistent across high-stakes arcs.

## Game-design hooks

- Class scarcity as raid-mechanic: an entire raid composition can pivot on one healer. Make healer-class rare in awakening tables.
