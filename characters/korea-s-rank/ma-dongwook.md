# Ma Dongwook

**Wiki:** https://solo-leveling.fandom.com/wiki/Ma_Dongwook
**Rank:** S (low-end)
**Class:** Strength-type S-Rank
**Guild:** Fame Guild Master

## Bio

Master of the Fame Guild. One of Korea's S-rank hunters but generally regarded as the lowest-output S-rank in Korea. His S-rank classification places him above all A-ranks but he is meaningfully behind Cha Hae-In, Choi Jong-In, and Baek Yoonho in raw output.

Strength-class fighter. Fame Guild operates as a mid-tier raid force.

## Abilities

- **Reinforcement (low-grade)**: physical augmentation similar to Thomas Andre's but at much lower output.
- **Heavy weapon proficiency**.

## Strength notes

- Bottom-of-tier Korean S-rank.
- Filler S-rank in raids; not deployable solo against high-tier threats.

## Development arc

- Background presence across most arcs. Stays in Korea for guild operations during international deployments.

## Game-design hooks

- Within-tier variance: even at S-rank there are weaker-S-ranks. Tier is not a flat category.
