# Go Gunhee

**Wiki:** https://solo-leveling.fandom.com/wiki/Go_Gunhee
**Rank:** S (former peak, declining)
**Class:** Veteran S-rank / Korean Hunter Association Chairman
**Korean:** 고건희

## Bio

The Chairman of the Korean Hunter Association. Decades-veteran S-rank hunter, founding figure of Korea's hunter establishment. Aging during the story but still an active S-rank by reputation. Mentor figure to many of the current S-ranks.

He is the first major casualty of the Monarchs War: killed by Baruka (Frost Monarch) in Seoul. His death is the moment the world realizes Monarchs are extinction-level threats, not "very strong magic beasts."

## Abilities

- **Veteran combat technique**: decades of refinement, but stat ceiling reduced by age.
- **Political authority**: Korean hunter establishment runs through him.
- **Mentor role**: trains and certifies new S-ranks.

## Strength notes

- Below current peak Korean S-ranks (Cha Hae-In, Baek Yoonho).
- Above the back-bench S-ranks (Ma Dongwook, etc.).
- Fundamentally outmatched by any Monarch.

## Development arc

- **Pre-canon**: builds Korean hunter institution.
- **Mid-canon**: notices Jinwoo's true strength via reawakening data, supports him quietly.
- **Monarchs War Arc**: killed by Baruka. Defining death of the arc.

## Game-design hooks

- Mentor death as escalation: kill the safe-feeling old veteran to signal the threat tier has changed. Time-honored shounen pattern, executed cleanly here.
- Aging stat-decline: rare in shounen, present here. Realistic veteran decay despite skill retention.
