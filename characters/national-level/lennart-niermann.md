# Lennart Niermann

**Wiki:** https://solo-leveling.fandom.com/wiki/Lennart_Niermann
**Rank:** National Level
**Class:** Hunter
**Nation:** Germany

## Bio

Germany's National Level Hunter. One of the original five from the Kamish Raid. Like Siddharth Bachchan, more of a roster presence than a feature character; the manhwa keeps him in the National Level conversation but doesn't extensively explore his kit.

## Strength notes

- Lower-end National Level.
- European front deployment during Monarchs War.

## Development arc

- Background appearance across International Conference and Monarchs War.

## Game-design hooks

- Slot-fill character archetype: every region needs a champion-tier character even if the main story doesn't develop them. Solo Leveling demonstrates restraint here; the world is bigger than the protagonist's screentime.
