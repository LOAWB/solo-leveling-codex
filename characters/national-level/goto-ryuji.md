# Goto Ryuji

**Wiki:** https://solo-leveling.fandom.com/wiki/Goto_Ryuji
**Rank:** National Level (Japan)
**Class:** Sword / Speed-type
**Nation:** Japan
**Guild:** Draw Sword Guild Master

## Bio

Japan's strongest hunter and one of the original National Levels. Speed-class swordsman with the unique ability to keep up with Magic Beast hypersonic combatants. His arrival on Jeju Island demonstrates the global S-rank hierarchy: even Korean S-ranks are visibly intimidated by his mana presence alone.

He is the highest-ranked human casualty of the Jeju Island Arc. The Ant King kills him, demonstrating that the Ant King operates above the National Level threshold. This sets up Jinwoo's solo of the Ant King as the moment Jinwoo formally surpasses every National Level.

## Abilities

- **Speed mastery**: matches the Ant King's hypersonic speed (briefly).
- **Sword technique**: peerless cutting precision.
- **National Level reserves**.

## Strength notes

- Lower-mid National Level by output, top-tier by speed.
- Killed by the Ant King during Jeju Island Arc.
- His death is the inflection point of the arc.

## Development arc

- **Pre-canon**: established National Level.
- **Jeju Island Arc**: deploys with Korean coalition. Faces Ant King. Killed.
- **Post-Jeju**: his death-by-Ant-King escalates the international threat awareness.

## Game-design hooks

- Named character death as scaling reveal: the audience learns the Ant King's tier by what he can kill. Use named-NPC deaths to communicate threat tier to players non-verbally.
- National Level with speed instead of brute strength: kit diversity at the same tier.
