# Christopher Reed

**Wiki:** https://solo-leveling.fandom.com/wiki/Christopher_Reed
**Rank:** National Level
**Class:** Sword / Wind-type
**Nation:** USA (second American National Level)

## Bio

The second of America's two National Level Hunters. Wind-type swordsman, generally seen as below Thomas Andre but firmly above any conventional S-rank. The Monarchs target him directly during the Monarchs War as part of their coordinated attack on National Level Hunters.

## Abilities

- **Wind-element sword authority**: sustained slashing AOE via wind blades.
- **High mobility**: wind-amplified movement.
- **National Level reserves**: massive mana pool from Ruler vessel status.

## Strength notes

- Lower-end National Level.
- Targeted by Yogumunt during the Monarchs War. Survives coalition defense.
- Above any Korean S-rank.

## Development arc

- **Pre-canon**: National Level via Kamish Raid.
- **Monarchs War**: targeted by Yogumunt's coordinated attack on National Levels.
- **Final Battle**: deployed in American front.

## Game-design hooks

- Second-of-nation structure: two National Levels per nation as a soft cap. Suggests rarity-tier design where each nation has a fixed number of slots.
