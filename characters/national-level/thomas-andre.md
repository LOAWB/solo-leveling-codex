# Thomas Andre

**Wiki:** https://solo-leveling.fandom.com/wiki/Thomas_Andre
**Rank:** National Level (originally #1 globally before Jinwoo)
**Class:** Tank / Reinforcement
**Nation:** USA
**Guild:** Goliath Guild Master
**Epithet:** Goliath

## Bio

The strongest hunter in America and one of the original five National Level Hunters. Pre-Jinwoo he was widely considered the strongest hunter alive. Massively built physical fighter, fights primarily with grappling and overwhelming brute force. His Reinforcement skill (a Ruler's gift) makes him near-physically-invulnerable to conventional damage.

His humbling at Jinwoo's hands during the International Guild Conference Arc is one of the defining "passing of the torch" moments in canon.

## Abilities

- **Reinforcement**: makes his body nearly indestructible against conventional damage. Tanks attacks that would level city blocks.
- **Grappling combat**: extreme strength, prefers wrestling and crushing.
- **Goliath Authority** (Ruler's gift): the source of his Reinforcement.

## Strength notes

- Strongest National Level pre-Jinwoo.
- Loses decisively to Jinwoo. The fight is brief; Jinwoo is on a different tier.
- Below any individual Monarch in raw output, but durable enough to survive Monarch-tier hits where other hunters would die instantly.

## Development arc

- **Pre-canon**: emerges as world's strongest after Kamish Raid.
- **International Guild Conference Arc**: meets Jinwoo. Fights and loses. Acknowledges the new world's-strongest.
- **Monarchs War**: deployed in international defense.
- **Final Battle**: holds American front.
- **Epilogue**: in new timeline doesn't awaken.

## Game-design hooks

- Reinforcement as binary defense: against physical damage you're invincible, against authority-tier damage the defense doesn't apply. Two-axis durability design.
- Pre-protagonist apex: the strongest character at story-start exists to be surpassed at a precise narrative beat.
