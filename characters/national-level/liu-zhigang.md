# Liu Zhigang

**Wiki:** https://solo-leveling.fandom.com/wiki/Liu_Zhigang
**Rank:** National Level
**Class:** Mage / Spatial-element caster
**Nation:** China

## Bio

China's top National Level Hunter. High-output mage-class with spatial-element specialization. His raw magical output is among the highest of any human ever, capable of leveling entire city blocks with a single sustained casting. Where Thomas Andre is the durability ceiling, Liu Zhigang is the damage ceiling.

## Abilities

- **Spatial-element mastery**: bends space for both offense (compression attacks) and mobility.
- **High-cast volume**: sustained-DPS specialist.
- **Ruler's gift authority** (variant): source of his elevated National Level status.

## Strength notes

- Damage ceiling rivals Thomas Andre's tank ceiling.
- Loses to Monarch-tier in 1v1 but contributes meaningfully in coalition.
- Survives the Final Battle without collapsing.

## Development arc

- **Pre-canon**: established National Level via Kamish Raid.
- **International Conference Arc**: meets Jinwoo, acknowledges him.
- **Monarchs War**: defends Asian front.
- **Final Battle**: survives.
- **Epilogue**: doesn't awaken in reset timeline.

## Game-design hooks

- Pure caster apex: Thomas Andre and Liu Zhigang sit at the same tier with opposite kits. Symmetric tier with asymmetric design is a useful template.
