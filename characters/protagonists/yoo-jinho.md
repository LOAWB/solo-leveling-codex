# Yoo Jinho

**Wiki:** https://solo-leveling.fandom.com/wiki/Yoo_Jinho
**Rank:** A (tank class, late B early)
**Class:** Tanker / Damage absorber / Buff-anchor
**First appearance:** mid early canon

## Bio

The second son of the Yoo Group conglomerate, Jinho is overlooked by his family in favor of his older brother. He hires Jinwoo as a personal raid escort during a mid-tier dungeon and the two form a mutual loyalty that becomes the founding bond of Ahjin Guild. Jinho serves as the public face Master of Ahjin while Jinwoo operates from behind the scenes.

Jinho is the everyman audience surrogate: low rank by genetic lottery, but committed, brave, and disciplined. His arc is about earning a seat at the top by association and persistence rather than raw power.

## Abilities

- **Heavy armor / Tank class**: absorbs damage. Specs into Defense.
- **Shield specialization**: blocks and counter mechanics.
- **Buff carrier**: by association with Jinwoo's army, often equipped with high-grade items above his rank.

## Strength notes

- Mid B-rank baseline, A-rank at peak after gear and combat experience.
- Out of his weight class against any S-rank enemy.
- Survives every arc by tactical positioning and Jinwoo's protection.

## Development arc

- **Demonic Castle Arc**: hires Jinwoo. Becomes loyal.
- **Red Gate Arc**: trapped with Jinwoo and his old guildmates. Witnesses Jinwoo's true power.
- **Ahjin Guild formation**: invited as Master. Establishes guild bureaucracy and PR.
- **Ahjin Guild Arc** through **Final Battle**: holds operational backbone of the guild. Rallies hunters during Monarchs War.
- **Epilogue**: in new timeline, would not awaken without the Gates. Implied to have a normal civilian life.

## Game-design hooks

- Loyalty-locked NPC pattern: fixed-rank ally whose value is relationship-driven, not stat-driven.
- Public-face vs real-leader pattern: organization design where the visible leader is not the actual decision-maker.
