# Hyena Guild (faction)

**Leader:** Brocky (originally Rakan's servant, brainwashed by Tiel)
**Tier:** Mid-Korean guild, secretly compromised
**Status:** Destroyed by Suho

## Overview

The Hyena Guild was a mid-tier Korean guild that became Brocky's operational base after he crossed into the human world under Tiel's brainwashing. Brocky used the guild's resources and members to harvest humans and hand them over to the Apostles. The members were either complicit or unaware (canon ambiguous in places).

In the light novel version, Brocky used Gray's Rakan-bloodline blood to forcibly awaken the Hyena Guild members and transform them into werewolves, building a Magic-Beast-tier paramilitary force. In the manhwa, Brocky's brainwashing extended to controlling the guild via authority alone.

The guild operated a Stardust factory as part of Tiel's drug-distribution network.

## Strength notes

- Mid-tier guild publicly.
- Magic-Beast-tier infusion via Brocky's authority and (in novel) Gray's blood.
- Destroyed in a single Suho operation.

## Development arc (Ragnarok)

- **Original era**: Hyena Guild presumably normal Korean guild.
- **Brocky takeover**: arrives in human world, takes over guild as front for human harvesting.
- **Stardust factory**: established inside guild operations.
- **Suho's intervention**: discovers the operation, destroys the guild and the factory, kills Brocky.
- **Aftermath**: distribution network suffers; Tiel's plan slows.

## Game-design hooks

- **Compromised guild as dungeon**: the guild's headquarters is itself the dungeon. Players raid an institution rather than a cave.
- **Werewolf/Magic-Beast infusion (novel branch)**: corrupting humans into Magic Beasts via blood resource. Conversion-as-mass-mechanic.
- **Faction takedown as plot pivot**: defeating an entire guild rather than a single boss is the arc-level reward. Group antagonist design.
