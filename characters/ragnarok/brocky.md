# Brocky

**Wiki:** https://solo-leveling.fandom.com/wiki/Brocky
**Title:** Servant of Rakan / Boss of Gwanak Mountain Dungeon / Leader of Hyena Guild
**Tier:** S-Rank tier individual (as Magic Beast)
**Korean:** 브로키

## Bio

Brocky was originally a servant of Rakan (Monarch of Beasts) tasked with protecting Rakan's descendants after the Monarch's death. After the Monarchs War ended with Jinwoo killing Rakan, Brocky escaped the battlefield with Gray (Rakan's heir) and went into hiding to keep the bloodline alive.

Tiel discovered their hiding spot and brainwashed Brocky into serving the Itarim. Under brainwashing, Brocky became cruel and arrogant, lost his loyalty to Rakan, became physically abusive to Gray, and even started drinking Gray's blood to make himself stronger. He crossed into the human world, took over the Hyena Guild, and used the guild to harvest humans for the Apostles.

Suho discovered the operation and engaged Brocky in a prolonged fight. When Brocky was killed, Tiel's brainwashing broke moments before death, restoring his original personality just long enough for him to die as himself. Suho later revived Brocky as a shadow soldier in his army.

## Abilities

- **Beast Authority (Rakan-derived)**: command over beast-class Magic Beasts.
- **Physical apex**: as a Rakan-line beast, peak physical combat output.
- **Blood enhancement**: drinking Gray's Rakan-bloodline blood made him stronger (corrupted form).
- **Shadow-soldier kit (post-revival)**: serves under Suho in the new shadow army.

## Strength notes

- High S-tier as Magic Beast.
- Loses to Suho but only after a prolonged fight.
- Above all human A-ranks; comparable to S-rank coalition.

## Development arc

- **Pre-canon (original Solo Leveling)**: Rakan's loyal servant.
- **End of Monarchs War**: Rakan dies, Brocky escapes with Gray.
- **Hiding period**: protects Gray as heir.
- **Brainwashing**: Tiel corrupts him.
- **Hyena Guild operation**: harvests humans for Apostles.
- **Defeat**: killed by Suho. Personality restored at death.
- **Post-revival**: serves Suho as shadow.

## Game-design hooks

- **Brainwashed boss with redemption**: kill the boss to free their original personality. Memorable mechanic for "good character corrupted by villain" design.
- **Cross-faction servant**: Magic Beast that originally served a Monarch, now serves the protagonist. Useful for retaining Monarch-era assets across timeline shifts.
- **Bloodline protector arc**: villain whose original mission was honorable. Adds moral weight to the kill.
