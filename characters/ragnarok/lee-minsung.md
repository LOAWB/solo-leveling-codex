# Lee Minsung

**Wiki:** https://solo-leveling.fandom.com/wiki/Lee_Minsung
**Rank:** A-Rank (post-corruption: Magic Beast / Shadow Soldier)
**Class:** Hunter / Public face (actor)
**Affiliation:** Fiend Guild Vice-Master, secretly serving Tiel
**Korean:** 이민성

## Bio

A famous Korean actor and A-Rank hunter. Son of Lee Wong-Yu, Vice President of Yoojin Constructions. Public image: kind, humble, smiling, charming. Actual personality: arrogant, cunning, narcissistic, fragile-ego, willing to do anything for power and fame.

In Ragnarok, Lee Minsung was a baseline A-Rank hunter when Gates returned a second time. His driver Lim Tae-Gyu awakened as one of the rare new S-Ranks and offered Minsung the Vice-Master position in his new guild, the Fiend Guild. Minsung accepted but festered with resentment at the gap between his A and Tae-Gyu's S.

Tiel found Minsung at his weakest point and forged a deal: in exchange for Minsung helping produce and distribute Stardust, Tiel would arrange Lim Tae-Gyu's death so Minsung's "fame would be restored." Minsung became Tiel's primary pawn in the human world.

## Abilities

- **Pre-corruption**: standard A-Rank hunter combat. Cunning and strategic but not exceptional in raw output.
- **Post-corruption**: transformed into a half-bee monster in the manhwa (angelic monster with curved blade arm in webtoon depiction). Enhanced physical stats, insect-themed abilities.
- **Shadow form (post-defeat)**: revived by Suho as a shadow soldier. Adds insect-themed skills to Suho's army.

## Strength notes

- A-Rank as human, mid-A by output.
- Mid-S equivalent post-corruption.
- Easily outclassed by Suho.
- Strategic value through his connections (Yoojin Constructions, public reputation, Fiend Guild access).

## Development arc

- **Pre-Ragnarok**: A-Rank hunter, famous actor.
- **Stardust deal with Tiel**: agrees to produce/distribute Stardust in exchange for power.
- **Hyena Guild fall**: his Stardust factories are destroyed by Suho's actions.
- **Paju Dungeon raid**: betrayed by Tiel, injected with high-quality Stardust at the nape, briefly transformed.
- **Confrontation with Suho**: tries to bargain by selling out Tiel's identity. Tiel hijacks his body before he can speak.
- **Defeat**: defeated by Suho, becomes a shadow soldier in Suho's army.

## Game-design hooks

- **Compromised public-figure villain**: a celebrity who is also a villain in private life. Useful for stealth-arc design where the protagonist must investigate without alerting public sentiment.
- **Pawn-redemption arc**: villain who becomes ally only after defeat and conversion. Same Arise mechanic used for Beru in original canon, applied to a human.
- **Resentment-driven antagonist**: not greed, not ideology, just envy of a peer's stronger awakening. Most realistic villain motivation in canon.
- **Connection-based threat**: his combat power isn't the threat; his rolodex is. Useful for non-combat-tier antagonist design.
