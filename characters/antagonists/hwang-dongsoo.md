# Hwang Dongsoo

**Wiki:** https://solo-leveling.fandom.com/wiki/Hwang_Dongsoo
**Rank:** S
**Class:** Strength / Tank
**Nation:** USA (originally Korean origin)

## Bio

American S-rank hunter. The older brother of Hwang Dongsuk (the B-rank antagonist who tried to assassinate Jinwoo in the Demonic Castle Dungeon). Carries a personal vendetta against Jinwoo for his brother's defeat. He is the primary antagonist of the Double Dungeon Arc (post-anime).

## Abilities

- **Reinforcement (lower-grade)**: similar to Thomas Andre's but at S-rank tier.
- **Brutal close-combat style**.
- **Resurrection-resistance** via stat allocation, requires multiple kill attempts.

## Strength notes

- Standard S-rank.
- Outmatched by post-Jeju Jinwoo even before Jinwoo's full ascension.
- Tries to leverage Jin-Ah as hostage; this fails.

## Development arc

- **Double Dungeon Arc (post-anime)**: ambushes Jinwoo, briefly captures Jin-Ah.
- **Outcome**: defeated decisively. Family safe.
- **Post-arc**: removed from spotlight.

## Game-design hooks

- Vendetta antagonist: B-tier antagonist with personal stake. Useful for mid-game arcs that personalize the protagonist's earlier kills.
- Family-as-leverage: brief kidnap arc as a one-time threat. Don't overuse this lever.
