# Hwang Dongsuk

**Wiki:** https://solo-leveling.fandom.com/wiki/Hwang_Dongsuk
**Rank:** B
**Class:** Strength
**First appearance:** Demonic Castle Dungeon Arc

## Bio

B-rank Korean hunter who attempts to assassinate Jinwoo during the Demonic Castle Dungeon raid. Acts on greed and resentment toward Jinwoo's apparent newfound power. Defeated by Jinwoo and removed from the active hunter scene.

His older brother Hwang Dongsoo is the larger-scale antagonist who returns later to seek vengeance.

## Strength notes

- B-rank ceiling.
- Defeated by Jinwoo without difficulty.

## Development arc

- **Demonic Castle Dungeon Arc**: assassination attempt fails.
- **Post-arc**: arrested or killed depending on adaptation.

## Game-design hooks

- Setup-payoff antagonist family: a weaker antagonist whose defeat seeds a stronger one later. Two-act villain design with a sibling/mentor relationship.
