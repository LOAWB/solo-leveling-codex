# Park Kyung-Hye

**Wiki:** https://solo-leveling.fandom.com/wiki/Park_Kyung-Hye
**Rank:** Civilian (briefly Eternal Slumber affected)
**First appearance:** Chapter 1 (in coma)

## Bio

Sung Jinwoo's mother. She fell into the Eternal Slumber, a dungeon-derived illness with no known cure, when Jinwoo was a teenager. Her hospital bills are the financial weight Jinwoo carries through his E-rank years.

After Jinwoo gains access to the System Shop he purchases a high-grade Holy Water (Elixir of Life) and cures her after 10 years. She wakes in the early Ahjin Guild Arc and the family is reunited (initially without the father).

## Strength notes

- No combat ability.
- Pre-illness: working mother of two. Post-cure: gentle, supportive.

## Development arc

- **Pre-canon**: contracts Eternal Slumber. Hospitalized.
- **Mid-canon (post-Reawakening)**: Jinwoo affords the cure via System Shop.
- **Awakening**: wakes after a decade. Reunion with Jinwoo and Jin-Ah.
- **Monarchs War**: kept safe under Ahjin protection.
- **Epilogue**: in new timeline never gets sick.

## Game-design hooks

- Eternal Slumber as quest gating: a parent locked in stasis until protagonist accumulates enough System currency to buy the cure. Translates to a mid-game milestone reward.
- Holy Water as System Shop endgame item: design-tier item that resolves a long-tail quest in one purchase, signaling protagonist has graduated past resource scarcity.
