# Sung Suho

**Wiki:** https://solo-leveling.fandom.com/wiki/Sung_Suho
**Rank:** Player class echo (Ragnarok-tier)
**Class:** inheritor of Jinwoo's Necromancer/Shadow line
**First appearance:** Epilogue / Ragnarok

## Bio

The son of Sung Jinwoo and Cha Hae-In, born in the post-Cup-of-Reincarnation timeline. He is the focal protagonist of the Ragnarok arc (light novel post-canon, partially manhwa-adapted). He inherits a fragment of Jinwoo's Player class and a portion of the Shadow Monarch's authority, including a weakened-form Beru as a partner shadow.

When dimensional invaders attempt to enter Earth via residual Gate scars, Suho carries the next-generation defense.

## Abilities

- **Player class echo**: reduced version of Jinwoo's leveling System.
- **Shadow inheritance**: a weakened Beru and partial Shadow authority.
- **Mana sense from mother's line**: inherits Hae-In's perceptive passive.

## Strength notes

- Stronger than any National Level in the new timeline.
- Weaker than Jinwoo at any equivalent age.
- Generational successor pattern: ceiling lower, but starting point much higher.

## Development arc

- **Birth**: post-Cup-of-Reincarnation, in the new safe world.
- **Awakening (Ragnarok)**: dimensional cracks bring the Player class back. Suho receives the inheritance.
- **First raids**: father (Jinwoo) operates from shadow. Suho is the visible protagonist.

## Game-design hooks

- Generational succession pattern: parent's class diluted to child, parent functions as silent mentor/protector.
- New-game-plus shape: world reset but inheritance preserved. The protagonist's child gets the codex, not raw stats.
