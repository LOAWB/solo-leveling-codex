# Lee Joohee

**Wiki:** https://solo-leveling.fandom.com/wiki/Lee_Joohee
**Rank:** B-Rank
**Class:** Healer
**First appearance:** Chapter 1 (Double Dungeon)

## Bio

A B-rank healer-class hunter, one of the survivors of the Double Dungeon raid that triggered Jinwoo's System awakening. She is Jinwoo's first close friend in the hunter world, the only party member who treats him with genuine kindness when he was an E-rank.

After the Double Dungeon trauma she struggles with PTSD. She quietly retires from active raiding mid-canon, choosing safety and recovery over staying near the volatile Hunter scene.

## Abilities

- **Healing class**: B-rank tier heals.
- **Combat-shy**: not a striker.

## Strength notes

- B-rank ceiling.
- Out of her depth in any post-Reawakening arc.

## Development arc

- **Double Dungeon**: nearly dies. Saved by Jinwoo's last action before his death-and-resurrection.
- **Post-Awakening**: distance opens between her and Jinwoo as he ascends.
- **Mid-canon**: retires from active raiding.
- **Epilogue**: in new timeline never awakens. Normal civilian life.

## Game-design hooks

- Trauma-driven retirement: not every party member can or should level forever. Some opt out. Models the cost of high-stakes adventuring.
- "First friend" archetype: emotional anchor early, emotionally meaningful when she steps back.
