# Ant King (pre-Beru)

**Wiki:** https://solo-leveling.fandom.com/wiki/Ant_King
**Tier:** Magic Beast / Threat-level Dragon
**Status:** Killed by Sung Jinwoo, then resurrected as Beru (shadow)

## Bio

The Ant King of Jeju Island. Apex of the magic ant colony that took over the island and rendered it a no-go zone for years. Hypersonic speed, intelligence above other Magic Beasts, capable of speaking. He kills Goto Ryuji (Japan's top National Level) and nearly kills Cha Hae-In before Jinwoo solos him.

After defeat, Jinwoo extracts him as a shadow named Beru. This is the keystone Arise moment in canon: the most dangerous magic beast becomes the most loyal companion.

## Abilities (pre-shadow)

- **Hypersonic speed**: matched only briefly by Goto Ryuji.
- **Sentient command**: speaks, plans, deploys ant subordinates strategically.
- **Execution finishing moves**.
- **Wings**: aerial assault.
- **Threat level: Dragon**.

## Strength notes

- Above National Level humans (kills Goto Ryuji).
- Below Monarchs (would lose to any one of them).
- Equal-tier to mid-tier Monarch armies.

## Development arc

- **Pre-Jeju**: emerges from gate. Takes over Jeju Island.
- **Jeju Island Arc**: kills Goto Ryuji, nearly kills Cha Hae-In.
- **Defeat**: solo'd by Jinwoo.
- **Post-defeat**: extracted as Beru.

## Game-design hooks

- Boss kills named character: signal threat tier through who they kill. The Ant King's tier was established by killing a National Level on-screen.
- Conversion-arc apex: this is the canon-defining "Arise" moment. The most powerful boss that can be converted, gets converted, becomes the army's strongest individual. Maximum mechanic-payoff design.
- Speech-capable boss: signals intelligence and adds drama. Most Magic Beasts roar; the Ant King talks.
