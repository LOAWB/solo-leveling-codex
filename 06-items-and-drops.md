# Items & Drops

Every notable canonical item, artifact, and drop in Solo Leveling. Organized by type, source, and game-design role. Built for system-design reference.

## Currency tier (universal drops)

### Mana Stones
- Drop from every defeated Magic Beast.
- Tier scales with Magic Beast rank (E to S).
- Primary hunter currency. Sized by tier; an S-rank Mana Stone powers industrial-grade applications.
- Used to pay for raids, buy guild gear, and (for Jinwoo) trade in the System Shop.

### Essence Stones
- Embedded in Magic Beast bodies.
- Crafting material, distinct from Mana Stones.
- Recovered by guild Collecting Teams.
- Used by craftsmen to forge gear.

### Gold Essence Stones
- Highest-tier crafting material.
- Rare, even from S-rank dungeons.
- Used in Ragnarok by Tiel to pay Lee Minsung for Stardust services. Implies high-tier guild deals operate in this currency.

## Rune Stones (skill drops)

Rune Stones drop from Magic Beasts and contain a unique skill extracted from the monster. When broken, the hunter learns the skill. Highly valuable. Even basic Rune Stones sell for large sums. Top-tier ones are nation-protected.

### Notable Rune Stones

- **Kamish's Rune Stone** - contains **Dragon's Fear** (mana-infused soul shout that drops anyone weaker than the user into despair-and-panic state, allies-affected included). Heavily guarded by the US Federal Bureau of Hunters.
- **Igris's Rune Stone** - contains **Ruler's Hands** (also translated Dominator's Touch). Telekinetic skill. Later revealed as a lesser version of Ruler's Authority, the true cosmic-tier skill of the Rulers and their Vessels.
- **Baran's Rune Stone** - contains **Shadow Exchange** (instant teleport-swap with one of the user's shadows regardless of distance). Foundational mobility skill for Jinwoo's tactical play.
- **Kang Taeshik's Rune Stone** - contains **Stealth** (full physical and magical invisibility). Notable: Kang Taeshik was a human, not a Magic Beast. The System treated him as a defeatable opponent for quest purposes and rewarded a Rune Stone anyway. Edge case in the canon system.

### Rune Stone game-design notes

- Skills as drops are an interesting design pivot from "skills as level-up unlocks." In Solo Leveling, you can extract abilities directly from defeated enemies.
- Only the Player class (Jinwoo) absorbs Rune Stones automatically via the System. Other hunters break them externally to gain the skill. Two distinct interaction patterns.

## Top-tier artifacts (story-defining items)

### Demon King's Longsword
- **Class:** S-Rank
- **Source:** Baran (100th floor of Demon Castle, also the Monarch of White Flames in dungeon-form expression)
- **Effect:** large damage bonus + creates endless lightning storms in a small area for AOE devastation
- **Wielder:** Sung Jinwoo (initially), then bestowed on Igris

### Black Heart (Ashborn's Heart)
- **Class:** S-Rank+ (cosmic-tier)
- **Source:** Ashborn (offered to Jinwoo during the Monarchs War climax)
- **Effect:** transfers Shadow Monarch authority. Replaces Jinwoo's heart. Marks his soul as the second Shadow Monarch.
- **One-time event.** Not a repeatable drop.

### Kamish's Wrath
- **Class:** S+
- **Source:** Kamish (legendary dragon, drops were collected after the Kamish Raid)
- **Form:** twin daggers
- **Effect:** can effortlessly slice through almost anything; cuts dragon-tier durability
- **Wielder:** originally Thomas Andre (he had no use, kept them in trust); given to Jinwoo who is a dagger-class fighter; Jinwoo's primary weapons through most of canon

### Orb of Avarice (Sphere of Avarice)
- **Class:** A-Rank
- **Source:** Vulcan (Demon Castle, 50th floor)
- **Form:** red crystal ball made of petrified blood from Vulcan
- **Effect:** **Desire for Destruction** - doubles the user's magic damage output. The custodian at the Hunters Auction valued its amplification at 100% (the previous best-in-class artifact craftsmen could only achieve 50%).
- **History:** Jinwoo considered selling it, decided to keep it, gave it to Tusk. After the war, Beru suggests forging it into a staff. Dwarves transform it into a dragon-head staff with the orb in the open mouth.

### Demon Sovereign's Earrings (Demon Monarch's Earring)
- **Class:** S-Rank
- **Source:** Vulcan
- **Effect:** stat boost (specifics not fully detailed in available canon)
- **Wielder:** Jinwoo (early Demon Castle to mid-canon period)

### Fragment of the World Tree
- **Class:** S-Rank crafting material
- **Source:** Vulcan
- **Use:** highly prized crafting material for top-tier gear
- **Notable:** suggests there is a "World Tree" entity in the cosmology. Lore hook.

### Holy Water of Life (Elixir of Life)
- **Class:** S-Rank
- **Source:** crafted via System after clearing the entire Demon Castle. Ingredients gathered across all 100 floors.
- **Effect:** cures any ailment including the otherwise-incurable Eternal Slumber. Does NOT restore HP; targets diseases and sicknesses only.
- **Use case:** Jinwoo cures his mother Park Kyung-Hye after 10 years of coma.
- **Scarcity:** unclear if ingredients exist outside the Demon Castle. Effectively a one-shot endgame item per playthrough of that dungeon.

### Cup of Reincarnation
- **Class:** Cosmic / out-of-tier
- **Source:** the Rulers, faction-tier artifact
- **Effect:** rewinds time 10 years globally. Undoes the Gates entirely.
- **Cost:** the world's memory of Jinwoo. Only Jinwoo retains memory and powers in the new timeline.
- **One-time event.** Faction-level victory condition, not a repeatable drop.

## Mid-tier weapons and gear

### Kasaka's Venom Fang
- **Class:** B-Rank
- **Source:** Blue Venom-Fanged Kasaka (Hapjeong Subway)
- **Effect:** poison damage augmentation. Strong against most magic beasts but rendered useless against opponents with superior physical durability (e.g., Cerberus was completely immune).
- **Wielder:** Jinwoo (early canon, eventually obsoleted by upgrades)

### Knight Killer
- **Form:** evolved version of Igris's primary armament
- **Source:** Igris's Knight-Killer transformation triggered during Red Gate Arc
- **Effect:** anti-knight specialization, significant damage and stat increase against knight-class enemies

## System Shop items (Player-class only)

Sung Jinwoo's System has a Shop interface that accepts Mana Stones as payment. He can buy items that aren't available as standard drops:

- **Holy Water of Life** (also craftable from Demon Castle, available in Shop after sufficient progression)
- **Stat reset items**
- **Skill books** (parallel to Rune Stones but System-vended)
- **Various consumables** (potions, antidotes, mid-tier crafting)
- **Quest unlocks** (e.g., Job Change Quest access)

The System Shop is exclusively Jinwoo's. Other hunters don't have this resource. It is the major "asymmetric reward" of the Player class.

## Daily Quest reward items

- **5 stat points per level**
- **Quest reward items** (rotating: Mana Stones, low-tier weapons, occasional skill books)
- **Penalty Zone "rewards"** if quest skipped: forced combat with a magic beast yields some XP and minor drops, but not equivalent to completed-quest rewards

## Gear sets and progression

Solo Leveling uses tier-coded gear progression. A hunter's typical progression:
1. **E-rank gear**: starter weapons, no enchantments
2. **D-rank gear**: basic enchantments, low Mana-Stone cost
3. **C-rank gear**: dungeon-rare drops, single-element enchantment
4. **B-rank gear**: rare drops, multi-property enchantments
5. **A-rank gear**: rare boss drops or guild crafting (Kasaka's Fang tier)
6. **S-rank gear**: top-boss drops (Demon Sovereign's Earrings, Orb of Avarice tier)
7. **Legendary / unique**: cosmic-tier (Black Heart, Kamish's Wrath, Cup of Reincarnation)

For Jinwoo specifically, gear progression layers on top of his System-given stat allocation, so his power scaling is faster than gear-only hunters.

## Game-design takeaways

- **Drops as skill economy**: Rune Stones turn enemies into skill libraries. Game-design implication: every named boss could drop a unique skill, expanding the player's kit through play rather than through progression UI.
- **One-of-a-kind artifacts vs farmable gear**: top-tier items (Black Heart, Cup of Reincarnation) are story-locked and one-of-a-kind. Mid-tier items (Demon Sovereign's Earrings, Orb of Avarice) are dungeon-locked but obtainable. Differentiate cosmic-tier from farmable-tier explicitly.
- **System Shop as Player-class privilege**: only the protagonist has this resource. Asymmetric economy is itself a class-defining feature. Game-design: the protagonist's class isn't just stronger; it has a different resource system.
- **Crafting depth via tiered ingredients**: Mana Stone (currency) + Essence Stone (basic crafting) + Gold Essence Stone (high-tier) + special ingredients (World Tree Fragment, Dragon's Heart) = layered crafting economy.
- **Holy Water as gating mechanic**: an item that requires full-dungeon-clear to craft. Models high-effort crafting goals. Translates well to "the BoP weapon you craft after completing every wing of a raid."
- **Item-as-loyalty-marker**: when Jinwoo gives Igris the Demon King's Longsword or Tusk the Orb of Avarice, the gift cements the bond. Items as relationship-mechanics, not just stats.
- **Counter-class drops**: Kasaka's Venom Fang is useless against Cerberus. Each item has counter-cases. Real itemization, not "+5 to everything."
