# Power System

Solo Leveling stratifies power across three tiers: Hunters (humans), Monarchs (ancient kings of monsters), and Rulers (celestial beings). Within Hunters there's a strict E to S rank ladder plus an unofficial National-Level tier above S.

## Hunter ranks (humans)

Mana output is measured at awakening. Rank is fixed for life with the singular exception of Sung Jinwoo (the Player).

| Rank | Power benchmark | Frequency |
|------|----------------|-----------|
| E | Barely combat-viable, often lose limbs in C-rank dungeons | Common among awakened |
| D | Handles low-tier magic beasts, basic combat skill | Common |
| C | Solid raid party member, specialized class (mage, tank, fighter) | Moderately rare |
| B | Leads small raids, strong personal mana reserve | Rare |
| A | Elite tier, often guild leaders | Very rare |
| S | Nation-level threat alone, capable of soloing S-rank dungeons | Extremely rare, handful per nation |
| National Level | Power equivalent to a nation's military | Top 5 globally |

Korea has roughly 10 S-rank hunters at peak. Globally maybe 50-60 S-ranks across all nations.

## National Level Hunters (the original 5 before Jinwoo)

Granted authority and power by the Rulers (most of them are Ruler vessels). Title earned through the legendary Kamish Raid. The original 5:

1. **Thomas Andre** (US) — Goliath Guild Master, strongest until Jinwoo
2. **Liu Zhigang** (China) — top mage-class output
3. **Christopher Reed** (US) — second American National Level
4. **Siddharth Bachchan** (India)
5. **Lennart Niermann** (Germany)

Sung Il-Hwan (Jinwoo's father) is also a vessel-class fighter near National Level. After Jinwoo's ascension he sits at the top of the human board.

## Monarchs (9 total)

Ancient kings of monsters. Each commands an army of their kind. All but Ashborn fight against humanity in the Monarchs War.

1. **Antares** — Monarch of Destruction, King of Berserk Dragons. Strongest. The final boss.
2. **Ashborn** — Monarch of Shadows, King of the Dead. Originally a Ruler, betrayed and converted. Passes title to Jinwoo.
3. **Baruka** (also called Arte) — Monarch of Frost, King of Snow Folk
4. **Rakan** — Monarch of Beastly Fangs, King of Beasts
5. **Tarnak** — Monarch of the Iron Body, King of Monstrous Humanoids
6. **Legia** — Monarch of the Beginning, King of Giants
7. **Querehsha** — Monarch of Plagues, Queen of Insects
8. **Yogumunt** — Monarch of Transfiguration, King of Demonic Spectres
9. **Baran** — Monarch of White Flames, King of Demons (lightning-caster, also called Monarch of Shining Lights in some translations)

Power gap: a single Monarch can level a city. Three Monarchs together can fight Jinwoo to a draw before his full awakening. Antares alone surpasses any other Monarch.

## Rulers

Celestial beings who fought the Monarchs in eons past. Originally created by the Absolute Being (their god) as soldiers. When they discovered they were being used as entertainment they rebelled. Ashborn was the only one who stayed loyal, was betrayed, and fell to become a Monarch.

The Rulers manifest in the human world via fragments and Vessels (humans they merge with). Sung Il-Hwan is the Vessel of the Sun's Hand. The Rulers grant authority to National Level Hunters as gifts.

Cup of Reincarnation is the Rulers' final move: rewinds time 10 years, undoing the Gates entirely.

## Magic Beasts (boss-tier)

Outside the Monarch hierarchy but threat-level relevant for game design:

- **Kamish** — Dragon, the only Magic Beast to threaten extinction-level damage. Killed by the original 5 National Level Hunters in a Raid that defined the National Level title.
- **Ant King** (pre-Beru) — Jeju Island boss. Killed by Jinwoo, then re-summoned as Beru.

## Shadow Army (Jinwoo-specific)

Shadow Monarch authority. Jinwoo extracts shadows from defeated enemies and resurrects them under his command. Shadow growth has no inherent cap; a strong shadow can level up via combat. Key system mechanic for game-design translation: this is a tempo and economy layer, not a flat unit pool.

Tier within shadows:
- Soldier: basic infantry, hundreds-to-thousands
- Knight: elite line, scaling from named hunters
- General: Igris, Beru, Tusk, Iron — solo S-rank takedowns
- Marshal: Bellion, Beru post-evolution, Igris post-Knight Killer awakening — multi-S-rank takedowns
- Beast/Special: Tank (wolf), Kaisel (wyvern), Greed (former knight commander)

## Power gap visualization

```
E < D < C < B < A < S < National < Monarch < Antares < Shadow Monarch (Jinwoo at peak)
                                                  ↑
                                     Ashborn at prime ≈ Antares
                                     Jinwoo post-awakening > Antares
```
